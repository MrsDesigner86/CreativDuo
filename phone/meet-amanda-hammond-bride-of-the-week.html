Meet Amanda Hammond: Bride of the Week

Written and interviewed by, Nicole Johnson

Meet our sixth #BrideoftheWeek, Amanda Hammond! Amanda and her fiancé Josh began their love story through their church and developed a deep friendship that eventually led to a relationship. They got engaged after Amanda prematurely found something special in Josh's car. Read Amanda's interview below:

 

Nicole: How did you and your fiancé meet?

 

Amanda: Even though we lived two miles down the road from each other, we ended up meeting at our church Six Mile Crossing Church of God. In August 2017, we became friends and then starting dating October 20, 2017.

 

Nicole: What attracted you most about your fiancé?

 

Amanda: What attracted me the most was his personality. We became best friends before we started dating. He was there for me and always made me laugh. We just clicked.

 

Nicole: How did your fiancé propose?

 

Amanda: He proposed when I actually found the ring in the glove compartment in his car.

 

Nicole: Are you planning on having a small or large wedding?

 

Amanda: We are having a small and simple church wedding

 

Nicole: When is the big day?

 

Amanda: Big day is October 20th 2018, which is our one year dating anniversary.

 

Congratulations to both Amanda and Josh and we wish you many years of wedded bliss to come!
