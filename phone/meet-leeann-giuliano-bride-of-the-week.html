Meet Leeann Giuliano: Bride of the Week

Written and interviewed by, Nicole Johnson

Meet our fifth #BrideoftheWeek, Leeann Giuliano! Leeann and her fiancé began their love story in college and share the same passion for travel. Little did Leeann know that her "happily ever after" would begin at the top of a mountain in Ecuador! Read Leeann's interview below:

 

Nicole: How did you and your fiancé meet?

 

Leeann: We met through a mutual friend in college.

 

Nicole: What attracted you most about your fiancé?

 

Leeann: Obviously he's a stud and a half, but he's one of the funniest people I've ever met. He's super clever and drops puns that are twelve steps beyond any advanced level of thinking I've experienced.

 

Nicole: How did your fiancé propose?

 

Leeann: We love to travel and were spending a week in Quito, Ecuador. We took the Teleferico cable car up to the top of the mountain (up more than 12,000 feet!). He wanted a "good picture" so he suggested we climb higher and higher (I think he wanted me to work for the proposal). After 15 minutes, but what felt like an hour of sweating profusely and attempting to catch my breath in the thin air, we found a nice spot with a beautiful view of other mountains in the distance. I was vlogging at the time, so he asked to hold my camera. When I turned around, he was holding the ring!

 

Nicole: Are you planning on having a small or large wedding?

 

Leeann: We're planning on having about 150 people at our summer camp wedding. Three days of booze, canoes, and I dos!

 

Nicole: When is the big day?

 

Leeann: September 1, 2018. Wish me luck. My to-do list is getting longer and longer!

 

Congratulations to Leeann and her fiancé and we sure do wish you luck with all that wedding planning! We wish you both many blessings and that your love will reach new heights as you grow old together.
