Meet Anika Wilson: Bride of the Week

Written and interviewed by, Nicole Johnson

Meet our eighth #BrideoftheWeek, Anika Wilson! Anika and her now husband Latate began their love story 20 years ago in college and then reunited decades later. They just recently got married but have yet to plan their dream wedding. Read Anika's interview below:

 

Nicole: How did you and your husband meet?

 

Anika: At Publix 20 years ago as cashiers in college. We lost touch and then found each other 20 years later.

 

Nicole: What attracted you most about your husband?

 

Anika: His eyes and his maturity. He’s a mans man.

 

Nicole: How did your husband propose?

 

Anika: He proposed asking me to “leap”. It was the sweetest thing. He told me how much I changed his life and how he wanted to do the same for me except we had to adopt each other’s pasts, which is extra complicated.

 

Nicole: Are you planning on having a small or large wedding?

 

Anika: We have to wait due to military life.

 

Nicole: When is the big day?

 

Anika: We are now married as of July 30, but I haven’t had the chance or determined if I will be able to have my dream wedding due to his status and obligations to his service. Pray  for me and my dream wedding.

 

Congratulations to Anika and her husband Latate and we hope you do get the wedding you've always dreamed of! May you both have many more years of married bliss to come. We also thank your husband for his service.
